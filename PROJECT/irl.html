About:

Indian Racing League is a city-based racing championship consisting of 6 teams competing in a league-style format. Each team comprises of 4 drivers, incl 2 Indian and 2 International drivers (incl 1 female driver). The concept of the motorsports league is to bring together auto enthusiasts and other sports fans.
